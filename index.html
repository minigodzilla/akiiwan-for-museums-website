Craftii

What if Museum Artifacts didn't need to stay behind glass?

What if you could re-make them and re-design them with your own hands?

What if you could take them home?

Craftii is an interactive touch screen + mobile AR experience that:

  • revitalizes museum collections
  • attracts new and repeat visitors
  • increases the impact of collections, without the expense of a major overhaul

Step 1: Touch Screen Crafting

  • A simple, highly-scalable touch screen experience allows you to “re-make” the artifact in front of you

Based on puzzle and crafting videogame design, you discover new information with each step you complete

An attractive dual-display kiosk can be repositioned every few months, giving you something new to collect on each visit

Step 2: Take Your Craft Home

  • Once the you have completed the craft and any customizations, a QR code lets you take it home

At home, you can explore it in life-sized Augmented Reality...

...and add it to other objects in your collection to build a tabletop diorama.
