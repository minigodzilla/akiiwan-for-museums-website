Craftii

What if Museum Artifacts didn't need to stay behind glass?

What if you could re-make them and re-design them with your own hands?

What if you could take them home?

Craftii is an interactive touch screen + mobile AR experience that:

  • revitalizes museum collections
  • attracts new and repeat visitors
  • increases the impact of collections, without the expense of a major overhaul

Step 1: Touch Screen Crafting

  • A simple, highly-scalable touch screen experience allows you to “re-make” the artifact in front of you

Based on puzzle and crafting videogame design, you discover new information with each step you complete.

An attractive dual-display kiosk can be repositioned every few months, giving you something new to collect on each visit.

Step 2: Take Your Craft Home

  • Once the you have completed the craft and any customizations, a QR code lets you take it home

At home, you can explore it in life-sized Augmented Reality...

...and add it to other objects in your collection to build a tabletop diorama.

The app lets you find other Craftii installations that you can visit on your next vacation and grow your collection even more.

Initially focused on revitalizing Indigenous collections and re-connecting them to the people who made them, their stories, and the land, the experience has a wide range of applications.

Benefits for Curators:

  • Refresh content without costly permanent installations
  • Encourage visitors to re-engage with older galleries
  • Call attention to specific objects within large collections
  • Combat “museum fatigue” with take-home experiences
  • Connect scattered objects to unifying story and history

Benefits for Marketing & Finance:

  • Stands can move throughout the year, visitors return to “collect them all”
  • Offers take home “value-added” that maintains connections outside museum hours
  • Both nudge visitors towards becoming members and members towards becoming donors
  • Motivates out-of-town visitors to visit to add to their collection
  • Extremely cost effective and scalable content and physical design
